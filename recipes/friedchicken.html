Fried Chicken

Description:

This recipe creates juicy, tender meat with crispy skin. This is perfect for a summer picnic.

Ingredients:

  • Bone-In Chicken
  • Flour
  • Spice Blend
  • Buttermilk
  • Peanut Oil

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken pieces in Buttermilk Overnight, covered in the fridge
  2. Heat 4 cups of Peanut Oil in a deep cast iron pan over medium-high heat
  3. While oil is heating up, take buttermilk covered chicken pieces and place into flour and season mixture and bread evenly
  4. Once pan of oil has begun to slightly smoke, lay in pieces of chicken gently
  5. After 5 or 6 minutes, turn chicken to other side and continue to cook for another 5 minutes
  6. Transfer cooked chicken to a cooling rack or a plate lined with paper towels
  7. Allow chicken to cool for about 5 minutes and enjoy!
