Lasagna

Description:

This is the best lasagna my grandmother used to make. It is simple and easy and great on a cool day.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Oregano For Garnish

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine ingredients in a baking pan in layers
  3. Place in oven for 2 hours
  4. Remove from oven, allow to cool for 10 minutes
  5. Cut into portions and enjoy!
