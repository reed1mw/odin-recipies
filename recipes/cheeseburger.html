Cheeseburger

Description:

Our favorite cheeseburger to make on the grill. Super simple, and super delicious!

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickle

Steps:

  1. Form ground beef into patties weighing aproximately 4 oz. each
  2. Heat the grill to medium-high heat
  3. Once grill grates have become hot, lay burgers on the grill
  4. Allow the burger patties to sear, do not rotate or flip the patties too early!
  5. Depending on your preferred cooking temperature allow the burger to cook these times:
    • For Medium Rare: 3 minutes per side
    • For Medium: 4 minutes per side
    • For Medium-Well: 5 minutes per side
    • For Well-Done: 6 minutes per side
  6. 30 seconds before your patty is done, place cheese on top to slightly melt
  7. Remove from grill, and place on bun with your desired toppings and enjoy!
